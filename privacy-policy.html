Privacy Policy

BT Systems Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who receive SMS notifications from our internal notification service.

We collect only the information necessary to provide our SMS notification service, which may include:

The information we collect is used solely to deliver automated SMS notifications related to eBay listing alerts requested by the authorized recipient and to support operation of the notification service.

Information Sharing

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the SMS notification service or to comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data Security

BT Systems Inc. uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to help protect the information associated with this service.

Your Choices

The authorized recipient may discontinue SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to a message (where supported) or by contacting BT Systems Inc.
