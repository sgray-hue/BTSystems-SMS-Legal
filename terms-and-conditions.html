Program Description

BT Systems Inc. provides an internal SMS notification service that alerts the authorized recipient when eBay listings matching predefined search criteria are detected.

This service is intended solely for authorized users approved by BT Systems Inc.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the number of matching eBay listings identified by the monitoring system.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless carrier and service plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, no additional SMS messages will be sent unless you are reenrolled.
