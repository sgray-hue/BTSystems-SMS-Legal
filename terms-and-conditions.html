Program Description

BT Systems Inc. provides an internal SMS notification service that sends automated alerts when eBay listings matching predefined search criteria are detected.

Notifications are sent only to the authorized company owner who has requested to receive these alerts. This service is not available for public enrollment or subscription.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the number of matching eBay listings identified by the monitoring system.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your wireless carrier and service plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Once you opt out, no further SMS notifications will be sent unless messaging is re-enabled by BT Systems Inc.
